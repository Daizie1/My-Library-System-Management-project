❓ Library FAQ

Each user can borrow up to 5 books simultaneously. If you need more, please contact the library admin.

Books can be borrowed for up to 14 days. After this period, they become overdue and may incur penalties.

Overdue books are logged in the system. You may be charged a late fee or restricted from borrowing new books until returned.

Yes, you can place a reservation. Once the book is returned, the system will notify you.

Use the search bar in the Book List section to find books by title or author.
